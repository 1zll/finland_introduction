Helsinki Cathedral

The contrast between the chalk exterior and the green dome is beautiful in this cathedral. It was designed by German architect Karl Ludwig Engel and completed in 1852. Originally called the Church of Nicholas, the official name was given to the cathedral in 1959. It still functions as the headquarters of the Evangelical Lutheran sect.

Santa Claus Village

Santa Claus Village is located in Rovaniemi, a major city in northern Lapland. Events are held throughout the year, so you can enjoy it even if you visit in the summer. Also, the scenery covered with snow is very beautiful and wonderful. You can actually send a letter from Santa Claus Village, so why not give it a try?

Moomin World

Moominworld is located on Cairo Island, across the lake from the port city of Naantali. The entire island of Cairo is a recreation of Moominvalley. You can take photos with the Moomins. You can purchase your favorite goods at Moomin Shop. There are also goods exclusive to Moominvalley! You can buy postcards and send them to yourself. Please do try that out.

Temppeliaukio Church

Temppeliaukio Church is a Christian association belonging to the Finnish Evangelical Lutheran Church located in Tooro, Helsinki, Finland. The Suomalainen brothers' design was selected in a design competition held after World War II. It was built using a special method of being buried in excavated rock and was completed in 1969. It is bright and has glass panels that let in natural light.

Porvoo

Porvoo is located about 50 km east of Helsinki, about an hour by bus. Although it is a small town, it has beautiful nature and historic buildings. The rich forests, lakes, and beautiful scenery attract visitors, as well as the traditional atmosphere of the old town and cafes. There are also plenty of outdoor activities and cultural events worth visiting.
